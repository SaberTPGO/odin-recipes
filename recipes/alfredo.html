Alfredo


Ingredients

  • Shrimp or fav seafood
  • 2 TBps Butter or 1 TBsp oil
  • 16 ounces of pasta
  • Parmesean cheese

Directions

  1. Heat stove, add oil/butter and saute seafood
  2. remove seafood, add white wine and burn off (Optional)
  3. add veggies of choice and saute
  4. add cream/broth and boil/simmer
